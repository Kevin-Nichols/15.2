1: let is a block-scoped variable and it is not hoisted. It can be reassigned like var, but it cannot be redeclared.

2: const block-scoped and cannot be reassigned or redeclared. It is not hoisted.

3: let can be reassigned but not redeclared. const cannot be reassigned or redeclared.

4: Hoisting is when a variable is compiled at the begining of running your code no matter where the variable was declared within your code.
